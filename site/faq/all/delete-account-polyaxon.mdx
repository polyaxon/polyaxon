# How to delete a Polyaxon Account?

## Polyaxon Cloud

You can delete your account from the Account Settings page. Navigate to Account Settings from the user menu.

If you are the sole owner of an organization, first transfer ownership to another user or delete the organization ([docs](/docs/administration/data-deletion#deleting-an-organization)).

## Self-hosted Polyaxon

If you are using a self-hosted Polyaxon instance, you will need to delete any data associated with your projects manually from your database.
