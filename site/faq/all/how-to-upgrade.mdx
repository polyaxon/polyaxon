# How do I upgrade my Polyaxon account?

You can upgrade via your Polyaxon Project Settings. Please navigate to your Organization --> Settings --> Billing.

You can find out more about the Polyaxon Platform and Teams plans on our [pricing](/pricing) and [enterprise](/enterprise) pages.

## What payment methods do you support?

We currently support credit card payments via Stripe.

## How can I change my plan / payment method?

Head to the settings page in your account and click 'manage plan'. You will be redirected to your Stripe dashboard in which you can manage your subscription and payment method.
