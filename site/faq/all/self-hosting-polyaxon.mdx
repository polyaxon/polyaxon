# How to self-host Polyaxon?

## Overview

Polyaxon is based on an open source project. The core of Polyaxon can easily be [self-hosted](/docs/setup) via our community edition.

The self-hosted version of Polyaxon is also available as an Enterprise Edition (EE) which is paid and includes access to certain commercially-licensed features and services that require a [license key](/self-hosting/license-key).

| Polyaxon | Cloud            | Self Hosted           |
| -------- | ---------------- | --------------------- |
| Free     | Developer        | Polyaxon CE           |
| Paid     | Platform & Teams Plan | Business & Enterprise Plan |

## Learn more about Self-Hosting Polyaxon

[Our self-hosting documentation](/docs/setup) is the most up to date resource for instructions and questions around self-hosting Polyaxon in a variety of environments via Docker.
