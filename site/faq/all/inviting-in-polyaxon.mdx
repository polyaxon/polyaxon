# How to invite co-workers to your Polyaxon Organization

## How do I invite someone to a Polyaxon Organization?

Head to your Organization's settings and click the 'add a new member' button under the heading 'Project Members'. Enter the email address that your co-workers use in Polyaxon. You can find more information about adding users to a project [here](/docs/rbac#add-users-to-a-project).

## I cannot join a Polyaxon Organization

### Are you using the correct email address?

If you have been invited to a project, make sure you are logging in with the exact email address that this invite has gone out to. If you log in with SSO (e.g. GitHub or Google), your email address might be different from what you expected.

If you click your name in the bottom left of the Polyaxon UI, you will see the email address we have on record for you.

### Are you the organization owner?

If someone else in your organization is manager, ask them to re-invite you (to your Polyaxon organization).

## Access Roles in Polyaxon

### What types of access can I grant to data in Polyaxon?

The basic roles for granting access in Polyaxon are: Owner, Admin, Member and Viewer. You can see the specific access rights granted to each role in the [Polyaxon roles and rbac documentation](/docs/rbac#roles).

### More about Role-Based-Access Controls (RBAC) in Polyaxon

Head to the docs to find out more about [role based access controls in Polyaxon](/docs/rbac).
