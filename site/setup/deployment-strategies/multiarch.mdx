## Overview

Teams deploying Polyaxon on-premise may have clusters with heterogeneous CPU architectures, such as a mixture of amd64, arm64, and ppc64le nodes.

All Polyaxon images are built as multiarch manifests and support multiple architectures out of the box. No special configuration or image tag overrides are required — Kubernetes will automatically pull the correct architecture variant for each node.
