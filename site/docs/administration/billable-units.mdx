# Billable Units

Polyaxon Cloud [pricing](/pricing) is based on the number of active users and the compute resources consumed per billing period.

- **Users**: The number of active users in your organization during the billing period.
- **Compute**: The total compute hours consumed by operations (jobs, services, experiments) across all agents and clusters.

Use our [pricing calculator](/pricing?calculatorOpen=true) to estimate your monthly costs based on your expected usage.

### FAQ

**How can I track my Polyaxon Cloud usage?** Organization owners and admins can monitor usage from the organization billing page.

**What counts as an active user?** Any user who logs in or triggers an operation during the billing period.

**Are self-hosted agents included?** Compute consumed on self-hosted agents connected to Polyaxon Cloud is counted toward your usage.
