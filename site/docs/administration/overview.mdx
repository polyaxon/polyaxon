## Overview

The administration section covers how to configure and manage your Polyaxon platform.

## Organizations & Projects

- [Organizations](/docs/administration/organizations/overview) — Manage organizations, teams, members, agents, queues, connections, and presets.
- [Projects](/docs/administration/projects/overview) — Manage project settings, access control, dashboards, and searches.

## Authentication & Access Control

- [Authentication & SSO](/docs/administration/authentication-and-sso) — Configure authentication methods including OAuth2, SAML2, and OIDC.
- [RBAC](/docs/administration/rbac) — Role-based access control for organizations, teams, and projects.
- [Organization Management API](/docs/administration/scim-and-org-api) — Programmatic management of members, teams, projects, and service accounts.

## Security & Data

- [Audit Logs](/docs/administration/audit-logs) — Track activity across your organization.
- [Data Deletion](/docs/administration/data-deletion) — Delete operations and project data.
- [Data Retention](/docs/administration/data-retention) — Configure retention policies for archived operations and activity logs.

## Configuration & Billing

- [Spend Alerts](/docs/administration/spend-alerts) — Set up alerts for compute and monetary thresholds.
- [Billable Units](/docs/administration/billable-units) — Understand how usage is metered.
