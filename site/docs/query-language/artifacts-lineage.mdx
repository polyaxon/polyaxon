## Query

field                                                   | condition
--------------------------------------------------------|------------------
`id` or `uuid`                                          | [string condition](/docs/query-language/#query-with-value-condition)
`name`                                                  | [string condition](/docs/query-language/#query-with-string-condition)
`kind`                                                  | [value condition](/docs/query-language/#query-with-value-condition)
`state`                                                 | [value condition](/docs/query-language/#query-with-value-condition)
`path`                                                  | [value condition](/docs/query-language/#query-with-value-condition)
`is_input`                                              | [bool condition](/docs/query-language/#query-with-bool-condition)
`run` (equivalent to run.uuid)                          | [value condition](/docs/query-language/#query-with-value-condition)
`run.*` (e.g. run.name)                                 | [value condition](/docs/query-language/#query-with-value-condition)

## Sort

field:

 * `name`
 * `kind`
 * `path`
 * `is_input`
